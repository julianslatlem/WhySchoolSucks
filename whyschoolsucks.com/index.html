11 Reasons why school sucks.

Made by Julian Slatlem.





1. School is making you less productive.

When you're in school, you don't have the time to be productive.
So if you really want to be productive every single day,
you don't have time to go to school.

2. School is boring.

Humans require mental stimulation to stay interested, especialy gifted induviduals.
So if the school doesn't meet the mental complexity of an induvidual,
they will feel bored.

3. School is disrupting sleep.

School usually starts really early in the morning, usually around 8-9am.
So if you're a night owl like me and most other people,
you're going to feel tired all day long.

4. You don't learn anything.

When you train for exams, you usually just remember a whole lot.
Then when the exam is done, you just forget everything.
So nothing really gets stuck in your head.

5. School is overworking you.

Overworking yourself is never a good thing to do, even if success is near.
But when you go to school, you don't really have an option.
That said, it's just bad for your health.

6. Too long school days.

A typical day in middle school is usually 5-7 hours, and it just gets worse from there.
For example, typical day in high school is usually 6-9 hours.
And it just goes on from there.
